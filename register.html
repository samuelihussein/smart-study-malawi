NSC Community Guidelines

Protecting our Northern Scholars

1

Academic Focus Only

This platform is for MSCE/JCE prep, career guidance, and regional culture. Please avoid posting political or unrelated advertisements.

2

Respect Each Other

No bullying or tribalism. Whether you are from Chitipa or Nkhata Bay, we are all Northern Scholars. Be kind to those asking "simple" questions.

3

No Exam Malpractice

Do not share or ask for "leakages." We believe in hard work. Any accounts sharing fake or leaked MANEB papers will be banned permanently.

4

Protect Your Privacy

Do not post your private phone number or home address in the public forums. Use the registration system to connect safely.
